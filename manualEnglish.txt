User manual for the AI:
1. Run the program
2. select whether or not you are labeling the most recent photo, or a batch of photos
3. click "Browse Folders"
    select the directory with the photo(s) to be analyzed
4. Wait until the progress bar is complete
5. You can opt to review all decisions the AI made, or you can view the decisions the AI was uncertain about
6. If you viewed the decisions the AI was uncertain about, you can now correctly label the photos
    When you are done labeling all the photos, click "Retrain"
    Wait until the progress bar is complete, then you may exit the program or rerun it on new data
